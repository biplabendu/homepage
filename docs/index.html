Biplabendu [ Billu ] Das

Email: biplabendu [dot] das [at] gmail [dot] com

I am integrative biologist working at the intersection of chronobiology, animal behavior, infectious diseases, and aging. I am currently working with Prof. Deborah Gordon as a postdoctoral researcher. For my dissertation, I worked with Dr. Charissa de Bekker, exploring the role of biological clocks in behavioral plasticity and parasitic manipulation of host behavior using the “zombie ant” system. Complete list of contributed works can be found below and on Google Scholar.
